What is MAN? What is WOMEN?

College Students' Sex-Role Concept and Its Factors

Independent Study, Advisors: Prof. Liezhong Ge, Prof. Yan Zheng, Department of Psychology, Zhejiang Sci-Tech University

Abstract

The present study examined the sex-role concept and its factors, especially gender, area and education by conductin a survey among over 2000 college students from four main cities. The results showed that: (1) The top five male traits are Masculine, Responsible, Staid, Mature and Tall. (2) The top five female traits are Gentle, Kind, Graceful, Attentive and Beautiful. (3) There is no significant difference between the views on the main traits of sex-role concept of male and female college students, but gender has a slightly significant effect on the minor traits. Area and Education has a slightly significant effect on both main traits and minor traits yet. (4) College students' sex-role concept is still coherent with traditional viewpoint.
