    About me

    I was born and raised in a small town in central China, where I produced lots of little artworks and dreamed to be an artist, while later I found out I was better at maths and science. Upon entering college, Software Engineering happened to be my major and luckily I fell in love with creating things by programming.

    During my undergraduate years at Fudan University in Shanghai, I helped to organize TedxFDU and was actively involved in design and entrepreneurship events in Shanghai, including design workshops by IDEO, Frog Design and Techyizu. Inspired, I realized that with a strong technical background, an artistic mind, hunger for innovation and caring for humanities, there are ways I could make an impact. So I joined the CS Master program at Stanford specialized in Human-Computer Interaction.

    My time at Stanford has been overwhelmingly exciting and rewarding. The HCI and design classes have provided me with valuable theories, principles and tools to do human-centered design. More importantly, working with the smart innovators around Stanford have sharpened my collaboration skills and inspired me in enormous ways. I’ve even tried applying design thinking to redesigning theater. Prof. Cliff Nass’s class has complemented my knowledge on interface design with sociological and psychological perspectives.

    Recently, one of my data visualization projects has been accepted as a poster by UIST 2013 and I attended the conference in St. Andrews, Scotland, where cutting-edge HCI research was showcased. Meanwhile, I’ve just finished my work on Stanford’s Solar Decathlon project designing an integrated tablet app to guide homeowners develop sustainable lifestyle, where I explored combining data visualization with behavioral design. During this summer, I worked as a mobile design intern at Mobiata doing UI/UX design and product ideation. Now I'm taking AI, Social Network Analysis, TAing Machine Learning, while doing several other exciting side projects.

    My other passions include music, modern art, popular culture, fashion and travelling. I'm a big fan of indie music (The album wall above shows some of my collection) and have been to various live shows / major music festivals in Beijing, HK, Shanghai, Dublin, Chicago and San Francisco.
